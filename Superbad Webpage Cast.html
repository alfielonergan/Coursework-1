Jonah Hill

Jonah Hill is an American actor, director and producer best known for his comedic work in films like Superbad, 21/22 Jump Street and The Wolf of Wall Street. Hill made his directorial debut with a film called Mid90s in 2018. The coming-of-age comedy-drama was well received by critics & skaters alike and the sense of nostalgia was praised. Also an Adidas enthusiast, Hill has his own range with the brand that launched in 2020.

Michael Cera

Michael Cera is a Canadian actor, comedian and producer most famous for his work in films like Superbad, Juno and Year One. Cera has also done voice work in films like Sausage Party and The Lego Batman Movie. In addition to acting Cera is also a musician, releasing his debut album ‘True That’ in 2014.
